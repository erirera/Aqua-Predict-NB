🚀 **Excited to share my latest project: an interactive AI Groundwater Risk Dashboard for New Brunswick!** 💧🌍

I’ve built a fast, interactive web application that transforms complex geoscience and climate data into actionable insights for private well owners. This project bridges the gap between academic AI research and public-facing environmental tools.

**Key Features:**
🗺️ **Interactive Spatial Mapping** built with Leaflet.js to track thousands of wells across NB.
📈 **AI Forecast Visualizations** using Chart.js to simulate LSTM-driven groundwater level predictions over a 1–6 month horizon, complete with 10th/90th percentile drought risk intervals.
⚠️ **Water Quality Risk Overlays** that combine geological risks (Arsenic, Uranium, Methane) with drought stress to highlight critical vulnerability zones.
📄 **Integrated Proposal Viewer** that seamlessly transitions between the original research document and the live, interactive data prototype.

The entire frontend is built from scratch with Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, featuring a premium glassmorphic UI that makes exploring massive spatial and time-series datasets both intuitive and visually stunning.
